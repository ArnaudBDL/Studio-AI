# Prompt Matrix

## Style clusters

- ambient, cinematic, textural, slow-build
- synthwave, retro-futuristic, nocturnal, melodic
- alt-rock, atmospheric, introspective, dynamic
- cinematic orchestral, dramatic, wide, narrative
- electronic pop, polished, hook-driven, modern

## Mood

- melancholic
- nostalgic
- tense
- hopeful
- intimate
- defiant
- dreamy
- triumphant

## Atmosphere

- vast
- nocturnal
- ethereal
- urban
- intimate
- stormy
- lonely
- luminous

## Structure

- slow build with evolving textures
- verse / chorus contrast with restrained intro
- linear progression without obvious chorus
- looping evolution with subtle variation
- multi-part movement with abrupt transition
- soft opening into wide climax

## Texture

- warm synth pads and distant drones
- pulsing arpeggios and gated drums
- fuzzy guitars and restrained rhythm section
- low strings, brass swells and deep percussion
- airy layers with minimal pulse
- close-mic acoustic texture with soft ambience

## Vocal settings

- instrumental only
- sparse whispered vocals
- intimate lead vocal
- distant layered vocal textures
- dominant anthemic vocal

## Lyrics tone

- abstract fragmented lyrics
- internal monologue
- bittersweet romantic tone
- existential imagery
- direct emotional confession

## Constraints

- avoid bright cheerful tone
- no generic pop chorus
- avoid festival energy
- no overly busy percussion
- avoid cartoonish orchestral tone
- no aggressive distortion unless central

## Assembly examples

```txt
ambient, melancholic, ethereal, slow build with evolving textures, warm synth pads and distant drones, instrumental only, avoid bright cheerful tone
```

```txt
alt-rock, intimate and tense, nocturnal, verse / chorus contrast with restrained intro, fuzzy guitars and restrained rhythm section, intimate lead vocal, no generic pop chorus
```
