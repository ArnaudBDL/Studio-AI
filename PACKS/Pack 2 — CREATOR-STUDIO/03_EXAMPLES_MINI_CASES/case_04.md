# Case 04 — Cinematic Orchestral Drama

## Brief

Créer un morceau cinématique orchestral pour une scène dramatique de film. Objectif: chaîne orchestrale lush, crescendos puissants, tension narrative. Public: compositeurs films, producteurs contenu dramatique.

## Prompt v1

"Cinematic orchestral dramatic, strings soaring, brass powerful, timpani, dramatic and epic. Build to crescendo. Cinematic score, movie soundtrack feel. 90 BPM slow, majestic, emotional depth."

## Problem observed

Résultat générique type "hollywood epic" sans subtilité. Crescendos trop explosifs, manque de breathing room. Strings surtraitées, moins de caractère distinctif.

## Prompt v2 (correction)

"Cinematic orchestral drama, epic but restrained. Tempo 88-92 BPM. Intro: solo cello or low strings establishing tone. Building: violins, violas layering in. Mid-section: subtle brass (French horns, trombones) supporting, timpani entering gradually. Crescendo: full orchestration (strings + brass + woodwinds) but not bombastic. Emotional arc over 4:00. Breakdown moments between phrases. Instrumental, no vocals. Orchestral textures warm and analog-sounding."

## Expected result

Arc dramatique clair avec breathing room entre sections. Strings chaleureuses (pas synthesized), brass supportif pas dominant. Crescendos organiques, durée cohérente. Valide si narrative musicale soutenue.
