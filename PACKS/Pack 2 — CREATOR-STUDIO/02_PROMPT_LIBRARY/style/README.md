# Style Library — Blocs réutilisables

Chaque bloc style peut être copié/collé ou adapté dans tes prompts.

---

## Ambient

**Bloc: Ethereal Spacious**
- Mots-clés stables: ethereal, vast, spacious, minimal, transcendent
- Erreurs à éviter: trop synthétique (ajouter "warm"), trop plat (ajouter "evolving")
- Exemple: "Ethereal and vast ambient soundscape, spacious textures, minimal rhythm, transcendent mood"

**Bloc: Dark Moody**
- Mots-clés stables: dark, introspective, melancholic, lo-fi, warm
- Erreurs à éviter: trop triste (équilibrer avec "warm"), perte de texte (ajouter "tape saturation")
- Exemple: "Dark and introspective ambient, warm lo-fi textures, melancholic but not depressing, gentle evolution"

---

## Synthwave

**Bloc: 80s Retro**
- Mots-clés stables: synthwave, 1980s, analog, warm, neon
- Erreurs à éviter: oublier "analog" (devient trap moderne), perte esthétique 80s (préciser "Prophet/Moog vibes")
- Exemple: "Synthwave 1980s aesthetic, warm analog synths (Prophet/Moog), neon-drenched atmosphere, steady vintage drums"

**Bloc: Darkwave**
- Mots-clés stables: darkwave, moody, cinematic, 80s gothic
- Erreurs à éviter: trop lourd (garder "cinematic"), manque d'énergie (ajouter "steady groove")
- Exemple: "Darkwave 1980s, moody and cinematic, synth arpeggios with reverb, dark but groovy"

---

## Alt-Rock

**Bloc: Indie Clean**
- Mots-clés stables: indie, alt-rock, clean guitars, dynamic, emotional
- Erreurs à éviter: trop pop (ajouter "edge"), perte de rock (assurer "electric guitars")
- Exemple: "Indie alt-rock, clean electric guitars, dynamic vocal delivery, emotional arc, rock energy"

**Bloc: Heavy Alternative**
- Mots-clés stables: alt-rock, heavy, distorted, driving, explosive
- Erreurs à éviter: trop metal (garder "alternative"), perte de structure (assurer "verse-chorus-bridge")
- Exemple: "Heavy alternative rock, distorted guitars kick in chorus, driving drums, explosive energy but structured"

---

## Cinematic

**Bloc: Orchestral Epic**
- Mots-clés stables: cinematic, orchestral, epic, strings, brass, dramatic
- Erreurs à éviter: trop bombastique (ajouter "restrained"), drums modernes (assurer "timpani/orchestral")
- Exemple: "Cinematic orchestral epic, lush strings, supporting brass, restrained dynamics, emotional depth"

**Bloc: Minimal Tension**
- Mots-clés stables: cinematic, minimal, tension, sparse, dark
- Erreurs à éviter: trop silence (ajouter "texture"), perte de menace (garder "dark", "ominous")
- Exemple: "Minimal cinematic, sparse strings, dark tension building slowly, ominous but restrained"

---

## Lo-Fi Hip-Hop

**Bloc: Jazz Smooth**
- Mots-clés stables: lo-fi, jazz, smooth, warm, vinyl, boom-bap
- Erreurs à éviter: trop moderne (ajouter "vintage"), drums trop clean (ajouter "slightly off-grid")
- Exemple: "Lo-fi jazz hip-hop, warm boom-bap drums slightly off-grid, Rhodes piano, vinyl texture, smooth and warm"

**Bloc: Dark Lo-Fi**
- Mots-clés stables: lo-fi, dark, moody, vinyl, introspective
- Erreurs à éviter: dépressif (garder "introspective" pas "sad"), perte de groove (assurer "steady beat")
- Exemple: "Dark lo-fi hip-hop, introspective mood, vinyl-saturated, steady boom-bap, moody but groovy"

---

## House

**Bloc: Deep Hypnotic**
- Mots-clés stables: house, hypnotic, deep, minimal, 4-on-the-floor, warm
- Erreurs à éviter: trop agressif (ajouter "warm", "minimal"), trop EDM (garder "house minimal")
- Exemple: "Deep house hypnotic, 4-on-the-floor kick solid, warm bass groove, minimal elements, hypnotic loop"

**Bloc: Tech House**
- Mots-clés stables: tech-house, groove, driving, synths, 128 BPM
- Erreurs à éviter: perte de groove (assurer "groovy"), trop minimal (ajouter "synth texture")
- Exemple: "Tech-house driving groove, locked 128 BPM, warm synth bass, groovy hi-hats, progressive texture"
