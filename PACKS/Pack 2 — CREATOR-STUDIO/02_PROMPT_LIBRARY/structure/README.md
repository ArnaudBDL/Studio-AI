# Structure Library — Blocs d'évolution temporelle

Chaque bloc décrit comment le morceau évolue. Mixe plusieurs blocs pour créer un arc narratif.

---

## Minimal Evolution

**Bloc: Sustained Loop**
- Mots-clés: sustained, looped, minimal variation, hypnotic
- Erreurs à éviter: trop plat (ajouter "subtle changes"), ennui (assurer "engaging loop")
- Exemple: "Sustained minimal loop with subtle texture changes every 8 bars, hypnotic and engaging"

**Bloc: Slow Build**
- Mots-clés: gradual, layered entry, organic, slow-paced
- Erreurs à éviter: trop lent (assurer "5-7 min max"), perte de tension (ajouter "building toward climax")
- Exemple: "Slow build structure: intro, layers added gradually every 16 bars, full arrangement by minute 3, sustained with subtle variations"

---

## Classic Pop Structure

**Bloc: Verse-Chorus**
- Mots-clés: verse, chorus, hook, repetition, catchy
- Erreurs à éviter: verses trop longs (max 16 bars), chorus non-mémorable (assurer "hook")
- Exemple: "Structure: 8-bar intro, verse (16 bars contemplative), chorus (16 bars explosive hook), verse, chorus, bridge (8 bars texture change), final chorus, 4-bar outro"

**Bloc: Bridge Variation**
- Mots-clés: bridge, texture change, breakdown, peak energy
- Erreurs à éviter: bridge ignoré (assurer sa présence), trop agressif (équilibrer avec "earned energy")
- Exemple: "Bridge section: reduce instrumentation (texture change), build back up with different instrument (synth pad or solo), create peak of emotional intensity before final chorus"

## Cinematic Arc

**Bloc: Dramatic Crescendo**
- Mots-clés: crescendo, rise, tension, climax, resolution
- Erreurs à éviter: trop explosif (ajouter "restrained"), pas d'après-crescendo (assurer "resolution")
- Exemple: "Dramatic arc: exposition (sparse textures), rising tension (layering instruments), peak crescendo (full orchestration), resolution (return to simpler texture), outro fade"

**Bloc: Tension Release**
- Mots-clés: tension, silence, breathing room, dynamic contrast
- Erreurs à éviter: manque de silence (ajouter "breaks"), perte d'impact (assurer "contrast marked")
- Exemple: "Tension-release structure: build moment of high energy, sudden drop to minimal (silence, single instrument), brief breathing room, build again with more intensity, repeat"

---

## DJ/Dance Structure

**Bloc: Intro-Outro Breakdown**
- Mots-clés: DJ-friendly, intro, outro, breakdown, main loop
- Erreurs à éviter: intro trop court (min 16 bars), manque de breakdown (assurer sa durée: 32-48 bars)
- Exemple: "DJ structure: 32-bar intro (build energy for mix-in), 64+ bars main loop (hypnotic, minimal change), 16-bar breakdown (half-time or filtered), return to main loop, 32-bar outro (fade-out friendly)"

**Bloc: Progressive Build**
- Mots-clés: progressive, element-by-element, energy arc, climax at 3:00
- Erreurs à éviter: progression trop rapide (spread sur 4+ min), climax trop tôt (placer à 75% de la durée)
- Exemple: "Progressive structure: minute 0-1 (beat + bass), minute 1-2 (add synth pad), minute 2-3 (add melodic element, build), minute 3 (climax, full arrangement), minute 3-4 (sustain or slight variation), minute 4-5 (outro fade)"

## Hybrid Structures

**Bloc: Verse-Loop Hybrid**
- Mots-clés: verse-driven, repetitive hook, minimal chorus
- Erreurs à éviter: confusion structure (assurer clarté verse vs chorus), pas d'evolution (ajouter "texture layer")
- Exemple: "Hybrid verse-loop: 16-bar verse with narrative or melody, 8-bar catchy loop hook (repeat 3x), variation (layer change), return to verse, repeat pattern"

**Bloc: Minimal Meditative**
- Mots-clés: meditative, timeless, subtle evolution only, 5+ minutes
- Erreurs à éviter: ennui (assurer "engaging despite minimal change"), structures trop claires (garder "subtle")
- Exemple: "Meditative minimal: establish foundation (first minute), very subtle texture additions (every 60-90 seconds), no dramatic sections, sustained warm atmosphere throughout, duration 5-7 minutes"
