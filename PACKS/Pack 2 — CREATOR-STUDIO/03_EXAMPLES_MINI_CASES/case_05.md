# Case 05 — Lo-Fi Hip-Hop Chill

## Brief

Créer un morceau lo-fi hip-hop pour playlists de focus/study beats. Objectif: boom-bap relaxe, texture vintage, ambiance café tranquille. Public: étudiants, producteurs contenu chill, créatifs.

## Prompt v1

"Lo-fi hip-hop chill beats, relaxing, jazzy vibes, boom-bap rhythm, samples, vinyl crackle. Smooth and laid-back. Study beats vibe. Instrumental. 85 BPM."

## Problem observed

Résultat trop mainstream « chill-hop » — perte de caractère lo-fi authentique. Drums trop clean/moderne, vinyl effect artificiel, manque de grooves décalés légèrement off-grid.

## Prompt v2 (correction)

"Lo-fi hip-hop chill, authentic boom-bap. Drums slightly off-grid (swung hi-hats, snare not on beat), warm vinyl texture (moderate saturation, not excessive). Tempo 82-86 BPM. Rhodes piano or upright piano jazz voicings, nylon guitar, upright bass groove, vinyl crackle atmosphere. Minimal samples, organic feel. Cozy and introspective. No vocals, instrumental. Duration 3:00-3:30. Structure: intro, main loop with subtle variations, short outro fade."

## Expected result

Drums en swing léger (authentiquement lo-fi), texture chaleureuse, groove minimal mais groovy. Pas trop « polished ». Candidat si ambiance café/focus soutenue du début à la fin.
