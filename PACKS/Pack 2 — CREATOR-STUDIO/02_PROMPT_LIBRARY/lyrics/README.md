# Lyrics Library — Ton, Point de Vue, Abstraction

Pour les morceaux avec paroles: fixe le ton et le degré d'abstraction pour consistance.

---

## Tone

**Bloc: Conversational Direct**
- Ton: parlé, conversationnel, accessible
- Grade d'abstraction: bas (concepts concrets)
- Exemple: "Lyrics conversational and direct, spoken naturally as if talking to someone, accessible concepts, simple metaphors"

**Bloc: Poetic Metaphorical**
- Ton: poétique, lyré, introspectif
- Grade d'abstraction: haut (images, métaphores)
- Exemple: "Lyrics poetic and metaphorical, introspective tone, coherent imagery system, grounded in emotion not abstract"

**Bloc: Storytelling Narrative**
- Ton: narratif, descriptif, cinematic
- Grade d'abstraction: moyen (scènes concrètes, enjeu clair)
- Exemple: "Lyrics tell a story, narrative arc with clear beginning/middle/end, descriptive scenes and emotions, singable hooks despite storytelling"

---

## Point of View

**Bloc: First Person Intimate**
- POV: "je", first-person singular
- Distance: rapproché, confessionnel, personnel
- Exemple: "Lyrics in first-person 'I', intimate and confessional, personal experience but universally relatable emotion"

**Bloc: Second Person Address**
- POV: "tu", direct address
- Distance: confrontationnel ou tendre selon contexte
- Exemple: "Lyrics address 'you' directly, intimate second-person perspective, creates direct emotional connection with listener"

**Bloc: Third Person Observational**
- POV: "il/elle/on", observation externe
- Distance: détachée, réflexive, universelle
- Exemple: "Lyrics observe in third-person 'he/she/they', reflective distance but emotionally resonant, universal themes"

---

## Abstraction Level

**Bloc: Literal Concrete**
- Actions, faits, objets observables
- Exemple: "Lyrics focus on concrete images and specific moments: doors, rain, her eyes, Tuesday morning, the coffee cup, exact sensory details"

**Bloc: Symbolic Poetic**
- Métaphores systématiques, symboles récurrents
- Exemple: "Lyrics use consistent metaphorical language: the house as sanctuary, journey as growth, night as uncertainty, developing one symbol throughout"

**Bloc: Abstract Conceptual**
- Idées, émotions pures, philosophie
- Exemple: "Lyrics explore abstract concepts: time, freedom, loss, identity, using images to ground concepts but focusing on universal truths"
