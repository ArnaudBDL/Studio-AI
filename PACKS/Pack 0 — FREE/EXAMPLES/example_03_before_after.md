# Example 03 - Cinematic

## Before

```txt
epic cinematic orchestral trailer music
```

## Problem

Le prompt pousse vers un cliche generique car il n'indique pas l'arc ni la texture voulue.

## After

```txt
cinematic orchestral, tense then triumphant, wide and stormy atmosphere, multi-part transition from restrained opening to full climax, low strings, big percussion, brass swells, no pop hooks, avoid cartoonish tone
```

## Why it works better

- progression dramatique plus lisible
- texture instrumentale mieux cadree
- exclusions utiles pour eviter le generique
