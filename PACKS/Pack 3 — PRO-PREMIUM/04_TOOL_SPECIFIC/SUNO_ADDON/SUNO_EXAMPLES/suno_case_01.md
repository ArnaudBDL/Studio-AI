# Suno Case 01 - Dark Hypnotic Club

## Brief

- Objectif: track club sombre et hypnotique
- BPM cible: 125
- Duree cible: 3:15-3:45
- Interdit: drop festival, hats trap, highs agressifs

## Prompt Suno (base)

```
[style: dark electro-house, hypnotic, club, premium, focused groove]
[no lyrics]
[tempo: 125 bpm]
[structure: 16-bar intro, progressive build, controlled peak, airy break, concise outro]
Tight four-on-the-floor kick, warm controlled bass, one repeating synth motif, smooth highs, dynamic contrast.
Avoid festival drops, trap hats, harsh brightness, over-compressed loudness.
```

## Resultat observe

- Force: groove solide et identite claire
- Faiblesse: reprise finale parfois trop dense

## Correction

Ajouter: "reduce layer stacking in final third".

## Validation

- [ ] Intro/outro mixables
- [ ] Kick/bass lisibles
- [ ] Aucun pattern interdit
