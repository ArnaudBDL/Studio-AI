# Suno Style Keywords

Liste de mots-cles utiles dans Suno, avec variantes stables et risques de derive.

## 1) Dark Hypnotic Club

- Stables: `dark`, `hypnotic`, `focused groove`, `controlled peak`, `warm bass`
- Risques: `epic`, `massive drop`, `festival` (derive big-room)

## 2) Cinematic Electro

- Stables: `narrative arc`, `textured atmosphere`, `controlled impact`, `dynamic contrast`
- Risques: `trailer`, `epic choir`, `huge boom` (derive cliche trailer)

## 3) Lo-Fi Lounge

- Stables: `warm`, `jazzy`, `mellow`, `introspective`, `soft drums`
- Risques: `chill` seul, `relaxing` seul (sortie trop generique)

## 4) Organic House

- Stables: `organic percussion`, `deep groove`, `subtle evolution`, `natural texture`
- Risques: `tribal` sans precision (derive stereotype), `aggressive` (perte organic)

## 5) Prompt helper blocks

### Energy block
`steady energy 6/10, controlled peak 8/10, no constant loudness`

### Structure block
`16-bar intro, progressive build, controlled peak, breathing break, concise outro`

### Exclusion block
`no trap hat rolls, no supersaw festival drop, no harsh highs, no over-compression`

## 6) Rule of use

- Combiner 4-6 mots-cles stables max.
- Ajouter toujours 2-4 exclusions.
- Eviter de melanger plus de 2 univers stylistiques dans un seul prompt.
