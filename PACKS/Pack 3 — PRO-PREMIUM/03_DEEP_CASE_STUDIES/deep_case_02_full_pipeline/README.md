# Deep Case 02 - Full Pipeline

## Contexte

Ce cas couvre un morceau cinematic-electro pour usage trailer court et streaming instrumental.

---

## 1) Brief Initial

### Objectif business
- Produire un titre utilisable en extrait video court (30-45s) et en version longue streaming.

### Objectif artistique
- Tension dramatique, progression claire, impact sans surcharge.

### Contraintes
- BPM cible: 110-116.
- Duree cible: 2:50-3:30.
- Structure: intro sparse, build, impact, retreat, finale.
- Pas de choeurs epiques envahissants.

### KPI qualite
- Intro identifiable en 10 secondes.
- Impact central net sans ecraser la dynamique globale.
- Fin exploitable pour loop ou cut video.

---

## 2) References Analysees

### Reference A - Cinematic moderne
- A garder: progression par couches, tension maitrisee.
- A eviter: hits trailer cliches a repetition.

### Reference B - Electro textural
- A garder: pulse discret, design sonore propre.
- A eviter: groove trop dancefloor qui casse la narration.

### Reference C - Ambient dramatique
- A garder: respiration, espace, sensation de profondeur.
- A eviter: lenteur excessive sans payoff.

### Traduction
- Energie globale: 5/10 -> 8/10 -> 6/10.
- Texture: sombre, granulee, cinematic propre.
- Rythme: pulse support, pas lead absolu.

---

## 3) Arbitrages

- Narration prioritaire sur impact brut.
- Impact unique fort plutot que plusieurs mini-drops.
- Espace et silence utilises comme outil dramatique.

---

## 4) Iterations Prompt (v1 -> v4)

## Prompt v1
"Cinematic electronic instrumental, dark dramatic mood, progressive build, emotional impact, clean production, no vocals."

### Observe
- Trop vague, sortie interchangeable.

## Prompt v2
"Dark cinematic-electro instrumental, 112 BPM, sparse intro, layered build, single impactful midpoint, controlled retreat, final emotional rise, no trailer cliche hits, no dominant choir."

### Observe
- Structure mieux definie, impact encore trop massif.

## Prompt v3
"Cinematic-electro at 112 BPM, narrative arc with restrained pulse and textured low-end. Use one clear impact moment, then reduce density for emotional contrast. Avoid oversized booms, avoid festival risers, avoid constant loudness."

### Observe
- Arc plus coherent, meilleure respiration.

## Prompt v4 (pre-final)
"Compose a dark cinematic-electro instrumental at 112 BPM, target 3:00-3:20. Structure: sparse 20s intro, progressive layered build, one precise midpoint impact, breathing retreat, and concise final rise. Keep pulse supportive, not dominant. Use textured atmosphere, controlled low-end, smooth highs. Avoid trailer cliche booms, epic choir dominance, and over-compressed loudness."

### Observe
- Version quasi finale, reste a affiner la fin pour usage cut video.

---

## 5) Post-Mortem

### Ce qui a marche
- Definir un seul moment d'impact a clarifie toute la narration.
- Les exclusions ont evite le rendu trailer caricatural.

### Ce qui a bloque
- V1 trop abstrait.
- Sous-definition de la fin exploitable video.

### Lecons
- Toujours fixer un plan d'energie par section.
- Pour cinematic: densite et silence valent autant que les elements ajoutes.

---

## 6) Prompt Final

"Create a dark cinematic-electro instrumental at 112 BPM, target duration 3:00-3:20. Build a clear narrative arc: sparse atmospheric intro (~20s), progressive layered tension, one precise and controlled midpoint impact, a lower-density emotional retreat, then a concise final rise suitable for video cut. Keep pulse supportive, with textured low-end and smooth highs. Prioritize dynamic contrast and section clarity over loudness. Avoid trailer cliche booms, dominant epic choirs, festival-style risers, and over-compression."

---

## 7) Checklist Release

- [ ] Intro identifiable en 10 secondes
- [ ] Impact central unique et lisible
- [ ] Retreat reel (densite baissee)
- [ ] Fin compatible cut video
- [ ] Duree dans la cible 3:00-3:20
- [ ] Export versionne conforme standard
