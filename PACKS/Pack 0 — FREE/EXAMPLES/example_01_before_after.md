# Example 01 - Ambient

## Before

```txt
make me an ambient song in space
```

## Problem

Le prompt est trop vague. Il ne dit ni l'emotion, ni l'evolution, ni la texture.

## After

```txt
ambient, melancholic, vast and ethereal, slow build with evolving textures, warm synth pads and distant drones, instrumental only, avoid rhythmic grooves and bright tones
```

## Why it works better

- intention plus claire
- mood et atmosphere dissocies
- structure explicite
- contraintes simples
