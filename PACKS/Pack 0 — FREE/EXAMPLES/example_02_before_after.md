# Example 02 - Synthwave

## Before

```txt
retro synthwave song with vocals
```

## Problem

Le prompt indique un style mais pas la direction emotionnelle ni la forme.

## After

```txt
synthwave, nostalgic and bittersweet, nocturnal city atmosphere, verse / chorus contrast with a slow emotional lift, pulsing arpeggios, gated drums, wide reverbs, sparse male vocals, avoid cheerful pop tone
```

## Why it works better

- meilleur cadrage du ton
- meilleure projection de la dynamique
- voix et exclusions explicites
