# Constraints Library — Exclusions par Symptôme

Quand quelque chose rate: cherche le symptôme correspondant, ajoute la constraint.

---

## Drums & Rhythm

**Bloc: Drums Trop Modernes**
- Symptôme: track sonne trap/EDM au lieu de rock/jazz
- Constraint: "No trap hi-hats, no 808 kick, drums must be acoustic or vintage-sounding"

**Bloc: Overdensité Rythmique**
- Symptôme: track surchargée, manque de breathing room
- Constraint: "No double kicks, minimal percussion, silence between phrases encouraged"

**Bloc: Tempo Confus**
- Symptôme: pulse égale ou flottante
- Constraint: "Lock 4-on-the-floor, no syncopation excessive, straight beat"

---

## Melody & Harmony

**Bloc: Synth Trop Brillant**
- Symptôme: perte de warmth, harshness non-voulue
- Constraint: "No bright/piercing synths, warm analog vibes only, mellow tones"

**Bloc: Harmonie Trop Cliché**
- Symptôme: manque d'originalité, sonore générique
- Constraint: "Avoid typical pop chords, use jazz voicings or minor 7 chords"

---

## Texture & Production

**Bloc: Trop Synthétique**
- Symptôme: manque d'humanité, digital-sounding
- Constraint: "Emphasize organic instruments: real strings, acoustic drums, analog synths with character"

**Bloc: Reverb Excessive**
- Symptôme: trop d'espace, perte de détail
- Constraint: "Minimal reverb, close and intimate production, dry but not sterile"

**Bloc: Dynamic Plat**
- Symptôme: aucune variation, monotone
- Constraint: "No compression aggressive, allow natural dynamics, breathing room between phrases"

---

## Genre Contamination

**Bloc: Genre Drift Vers Pop**
- Symptôme: ambient vire pop synthétique, rock vire chart pop
- Constraint: "No major chorus hooks, no bright vocals, no polished production"

**Bloc: Genre Drift Vers EDM**
- Symptôme: ambient vire EDM, house vire festival trap
- Constraint: "No drop, no build-to-drop structure, no aggressive bass swell, maintain hypnotic not climactic"

**Bloc: Genre Drift Vers Cinematic**
- Symptôme: rock vire orchestral movie trailer, minimal vire epic
- Constraint: "No orchestral strings overwhelming, no reverb cathedral, stay intimate"

---

## Duration & Pacing

**Bloc: Track Trop Court**
- Symptôme: setup incomplet, manque de développement
- Constraint: "Minimum 3:00 duration, allow full arc not just intro"

**Bloc: Track Trop Long**
- Symptôme: répétition excessive, ennui à la fin
- Constraint: "Keep under 4:00 unless meditative format, edit ruthlessly"
