# Example 03 - Alt-Rock

## Intention

Create an introspective alt-rock song with emotional lift but no arena-rock cliche.

## Prompt

```txt
alt-rock, atmospheric, introspective, modern, intimate and tense, nocturnal, restrained verse opening into a wider and more emotional chorus, fuzzy guitars and restrained rhythm section, intimate lead vocal, no generic pop chorus
```

## Variants

```txt
alt-rock, melancholic, intimate, linear progression without obvious chorus, close-mic guitar texture and soft ambience, sparse vocals, avoid polished radio sheen
```

```txt
alt-rock, defiant but contained, urban and stormy, multi-part form with a clear transition into a heavier second section, distorted guitars with controlled drums, dominant vocal, avoid stadium-rock cliches
```

## Si l'IA derape

Si le refrain sonne trop grand public, reduis les marqueurs pop et precise une texture plus rugueuse.
