# Example 02 - Synthwave

## Intention

Create a nostalgic synthwave song that feels nocturnal rather than euphoric.

## Prompt

```txt
synthwave, retro-futuristic, nocturnal, melodic, nostalgic and bittersweet, verse / chorus contrast with restrained intro, pulsing arpeggios and gated drums, intimate lead vocal, avoid cheerful pop tone
```

## Variants

```txt
synthwave, nostalgic, urban, looping evolution with subtle variation, pulsing arpeggios and wide reverb, sparse male vocals, avoid festival energy
```

```txt
synthwave, dreamy, nocturnal, soft opening into wide climax, layered synth pads and delayed lead lines, distant layered vocal textures, no aggressive drums
```

## Si l'IA derape

Si le morceau devient trop radio-pop, retire des hooks et renforce l'atmosphere nocturne.
