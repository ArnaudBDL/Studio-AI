# Case 06 — House Dance Hypnotic

## Brief

Créer un morceau house dansant avec groove hypnotique. Objectif: kick solide, bass groovy, rythme entraînant, progression minimaliste. Public: DJs, producteurs dance, playlists club.

## Prompt v1

"House dance hypnotic, 4-on-the-floor kick, deep bass, groovy, club vibe. Energetic and hypnotic. Trance-like build. Synths and beats. 128 BPM. Dancefloor ready, no vocals."

## Problem observed

Résultat trop agressif/EDM-ish — perte de sensibilité house minimaliste. Kick/bass en avant sans subtilité, manque de progression organique, saturé en éléments.

## Prompt v2 (correction)

"House hypnotic minimal, 128 BPM locked. Kick: solid 4-on-the-floor, punchy not boomy. Bass: synth bass or electric bass, groovy and warm, minimal filter sweeps. Drums: tight closed hi-hats (swing subtle), kick, snare on 2&4. Elements introduced gradually: first 1 min bass+kick, then hi-hats, then pad/synth texture (warm, not bright). Minimal vocal chops (optional, 1-2 phrases max). Structure: intro, main hypnotic loop (minimal variation), break/filter sweep section, main loop return, outro. Duration 4:30-5:30. Atmosphere: deep, hypnotic, club-ready but with warmth."

## Expected result

Groove hypnotique soutenu sans surcharge. Progression lente mais palpable. Bass distincte, kick solide, hi-hats serrés. Pas de drop explosif. Candidat si loop minimaliste engageante sur 4+ minutes.
