# Deep Case 03 - V2 Experimental Pipeline

## Positionnement

Cas "V2": exploration controlee d'un hybride lofi-breakbeat melodique, avec objectif de conserver l'accessibilite tout en injectant une signature plus originale.

---

## 1) Brief Initial

- Mood: nocturne, melancolique, mais energique.
- BPM: 92-96.
- Duree: 2:40-3:10.
- Usage: streaming + reels courts.
- Interdit: derive lo-fi tropee (vinyl noise permanent, accords trop attendus).

---

## 2) References Analysees

- Ref A (lofi): chaleur, humanisation, proximite.
- Ref B (breakbeat): mouvement rythmique, relance d'energie.
- Ref C (indie electronica): melodie simple mais signature forte.

Traduction:

- Garder la chaleur de A
- Garder l'elan rythmique de B
- Garder la memorisation melodique de C
- Eviter la caricature de chacun

---

## 3) Arbitrages

- Priorite groove sur complexite harmonique
- Priorite identite melodique sur texture gadget
- Priorite lisibilite mix sur surcharge creative

---

## 4) Iterations Prompt

## v1
"Lo-fi breakbeat hybrid, emotional night vibe, warm texture, memorable melody."

Observe: rendu trop flou, manque de structure.

## v2
"Lo-fi and breakbeat hybrid at 94 BPM, warm and intimate tone, moving drums with humanized timing, concise memorable lead motif, no overdone vinyl crackle."

Observe: meilleur groove, melodie encore trop neutre.

## v3
"At 94 BPM, create a nocturnal lo-fi breakbeat track with a distinct 4-note lead motif, warm low-mid body, and dynamic drum movement. Keep arrangement concise, avoid cliché lo-fi noise layers, avoid muddy low-end, avoid repetitive static loops."

Observe: direction validee, manque un vrai break central.

## v4 (pre-final)
"Create a 94 BPM nocturnal lo-fi breakbeat hybrid (2:45-3:05) with warm intimate texture, one distinct 4-note melodic motif, dynamic but controlled drum movement, and a clear mid-track break for contrast. Keep low-end clean and highs soft. Avoid cliché vinyl-noise overload, muddy bass, static looping, and over-polished pop sheen."

Observe: sortie exploitable et differenciante.

---

## 5) Prompt Final

"Produce a nocturnal lo-fi breakbeat hybrid at 94 BPM, target 2:45-3:05. Build around one distinctive 4-note melodic motif, warm intimate texture, and controlled rhythmic movement with humanized groove. Include a clear contrasting mid-track break, then a focused return. Keep low-end clean, highs soft, and arrangement concise. Avoid cliché vinyl crackle overuse, muddy bass build-up, static repetitive looping, and glossy pop polish. Prioritize emotional clarity, groove readability, and recognizable identity."

---

## 6) Checklist Release

- [ ] Motif 4 notes memorisable et identifiable
- [ ] Break central contrastant
- [ ] Bas propre (pas de boue)
- [ ] Duree 2:45-3:05 respectee
- [ ] Export versionne et nommage conforme
