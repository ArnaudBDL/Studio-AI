# Case 03 — Alt-Rock Energetic

## Brief

Créer un morceau alt-rock énergique avec structure claire (verse/chorus/bridge). Objectif: guitars croquantes, drums solides, dynamic range marquée. Public: musiciens rock indé, playlists alt contemporaines.

## Prompt v1

"Alt-rock energetic, powerful guitars, driving drums, rock vocals, indie energy. Verse soft, chorus big. Dynamic range. 120 BPM. Rock band sound, distorted guitars, emotional delivery."

## Problem observed

Résultat trop "generic alt-rock" — manque de caractère distinctif. Verses pas assez développés, transition verse→chorus trop abrupte. Vocals surdominent les instruments.

## Prompt v2 (correction)

"Alt-rock energetic, indie vibe. Verse: clean guitars, light touch drums, contemplative mood. Chorus: distorted guitars kick in, bigger drums, hook-driven. Bridge: texture change (add synth pad or guitar effect). Vocals emotional but balanced with instruments. Tempo 118 BPM. Structure: verse-chorus-verse-chorus-bridge-chorus-outro. Acoustic drums, electric guitars, modern rock tone but classic alt sensibility. Duration 3:40."

## Expected result

Structure verse/chorus/bridge claire et progressive. Guitares distinctes (clean vs distorted). Dynamics naturelles, pas de compression excessive. Vocal performance équilibrée. Candidat si arc émotionnel cohérent et distinctif.
