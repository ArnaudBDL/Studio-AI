# Prompt Matrix Lite

Choisis 1 element par axe, puis assemble une premiere version simple.

## Mood

- melancholic
- hopeful
- tense
- dreamy
- triumphant

## Atmosphere

- vast
- intimate
- ethereal
- nocturnal
- cinematic

## Structure

- slow build
- linear progression
- verse / chorus contrast
- looping evolution
- multi-part transition

## Style

- ambient
- synthwave
- alt-rock
- cinematic orchestral
- electronic pop

## Texture

- warm pads and soft layers
- dense distorted wall
- minimal percussion and drones
- pulsing arpeggios and wide reverb
- organic acoustic texture

## Assembly formula

```txt
[style], [mood], [atmosphere], [structure], [texture], [vocals], [constraints]
```

## Example

```txt
ambient, melancholic, vast, slow build, warm pads and soft layers, instrumental only, avoid bright rhythm and pop hooks
```
